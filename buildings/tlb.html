Tioga Library Building (TLB)

About

Address

1907 Jefferson Avenue

Info

True to its name, the Tioga Library Building contains the UW Tacoma Library's collection and services. It is also part of the Learning Commons (with the Snoqualmie Building) that includes the Writing Center. On the 3rd floor, you will find the Veteran and Military Resource Center and the VIBE Business Incubator.

This is a great place for quiet studying or some great views from the 4th floor.
