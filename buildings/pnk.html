Pinkerton (PNK)

About

Pinkerton is technically the oldest building on any UW campus. Originally constructed as a hotel to serve the Union Station train depot in 1889, it is one year older than Denny Hall on the Seattle campus.

Along with classrooms and the GIS Lab, the Pinkerton Building is home to the School of Urban Studies.
