Mattress Factory (MAT)

About

The Mattress Factory is where most students start their UW Tacoma journey -- the Office of Admissions is located on the 2nd floor. A number of other student services also make their home here including the Office of the Registrar, Financial Aid, Disability Resources, Career Development and Psychological & Wellness Services. The Dawghouse Student Lounge is located on the first floor.
