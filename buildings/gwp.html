Garretson Woodruff & Pratt (GWP)

About

One of the original warehouse buildings to be renovated for the UW Tacoma campus, GWP is home to University Academic Advising and the Office of Global Affairs along with classrooms and the University Bookstore. A four-story atrium includes spaces to study or hang out.
